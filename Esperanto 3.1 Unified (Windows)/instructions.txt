Tested on Windows 11 Pro, as of Sept. 20, 2024.

1. Click on the installer for your platform. For most people, this will be the "amd64" program.

2. If a security warning appears, click "Run".

3. At the UAC prompt, enter the appropriate administrative credentials.

4. Once "Esperanto 3.1 Unified" has installed, check the Taskbar at the lower-right for the Language Bar to select "EN-US: Esperanto".
Note that here, en-US refers to the base key layout, while Esperanto refers to the modified layout for Esperanto input.
