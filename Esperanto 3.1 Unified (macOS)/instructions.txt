Tested on macOS Ventura, as of Sept. 20, 2024.

1. Copy and open "Esperanto 3.1 Unified.dmg" to your Desktop.

2. Open the "Keyboard Installer" app.

3. Drag and drop the "Esperanto.keylayout" file into the "Keyboard Installer" app window.

4. Click on "Install for current user (recommended)".

5. Once the installation has completed, open System Preferences.

6. Scroll down to "Keyboard", then click on "Edit..." next to Input Sources under Text Input.

7. Press the "+" button at the bottom-left, then in the left-hand pane scroll down to "Others".

8. Select "Esperanto", then click "Add". The Input Selector will appear at the top-left in the Finder bar.
